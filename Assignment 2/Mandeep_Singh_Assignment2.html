National Preserved Areas & Environment Issues

Protected areas play a critical role in conserving biodiversity, managing ecosystems, and ensuring the survival of endangered species. As climate change accelerates and human activities continue to impact natural habitats, protected areas have become more essential than ever. They provide a refuge for wildlife, preserve natural landscapes, and offer sustainable tourism opportunities.
